Stafetten

Stafetten is an ongoing combined art and music project by Natalia Chuvashova and Islandery. It started 2018 and the idea is a kind of ping pong whisper game where each art piece inspires a song, which in turn inspires a new piece of art.

All artwork under this section is ©Natalia Chuvashova 2018-2019.
